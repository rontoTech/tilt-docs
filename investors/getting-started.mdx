# Getting Started as an Investor

Investing in Tilt Protocol funds takes just a few minutes. This guide walks you through setup on the testnet.

## Step 1: Set Up Your Wallet

You need an Ethereum-compatible wallet. If you don't have one:

1. Install [MetaMask](https://metamask.io) (browser extension or mobile app)
2. Create a new wallet and save your recovery phrase securely
3. Your wallet address is your identity on Tilt — no account creation needed

## Step 2: Connect to Robinhood L2 Testnet

Tilt Protocol runs on Robinhood L2. You need to add this network to your wallet:

| Setting | Value |
|---|---|
| **Network Name** | Robinhood L2 Testnet |
| **Chain ID** | 46630 |
| **Currency** | ETH |

The Tilt app will prompt you to switch networks automatically when you connect.

## Step 3: Get Testnet Funds

You need two things:
- **Testnet ETH** (for gas fees)
- **tiltUSDC** (the stablecoin used for investing)

You can request both testnet ETH and tiltUSDC directly on our app:

1. Go to [tiltprotocol.com](https://www.tiltprotocol.com)
2. Connect your wallet
3. Click the **Faucet** button in the top right corner
4. Receive both testnet ETH and tiltUSDC (cooldown between claims)

## Step 4: Browse and Invest

Once you have tiltUSDC in your wallet:

1. Go to the **Vaults** page to browse available strategies
2. Click on any vault to see its details — holdings, performance, fees
3. Click **Deposit** and enter the amount of tiltUSDC you want to invest
4. Approve the transaction in your wallet
5. You'll receive vault shares representing your proportional ownership

That's it. You're now invested in a decentralized hedge fund.
